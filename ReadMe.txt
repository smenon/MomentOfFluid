    H. T. Ahn, M. Shashkov,
    Multi-material interface reconstruction
    on generalized polyhedral meshes,
    Journal of Computational Physics,
    Volume 226, Issue 2, 1 October 2007, Pages 2096-2132,
    ISSN 0021-9991, 10.1016/j.jcp.2007.06.033.
